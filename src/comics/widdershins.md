Widdershins is a series of Victorian-era adventure comics, set in the fictional town of Widdershins, West Yorkshire- England's magical epicentre, home to bounty hunters, failed wizards, stage magicians, and more besides..!
It's lighthearted, fun, and often comical, with a hefty 1000+ page archive!
